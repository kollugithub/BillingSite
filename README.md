# BillingSite
Super Market Billing Management System
super market billing system is a application by which we can do fast billing,maintaining stock, price of items and discounts etc.
Work flow 
Work in the Supermarket will be done in thefollowing way:
1. The product will come in the store.
2. The Administrator will enter the information of theproduct in database and price and discountavailable for each product.
4. The customer will come and take the basket withhim/her and choose the product and take it to thecounter.
5. The bill calculating operator will enter the productnumber then it will show its information and priceand the bill will be calculated and total payment willshown.
6. Customer will pay for the products.7. All the products will be packed and delivered tothe customer.
